Federated Data-Sharing & Privacy-by-Design Principles

This document defines the minimum-data-sharing philosophy of Project ILM: exchange only the minimum information required for computational interoperability and scientific reproducibility, keep primary sociolinguistic data under local and community governance, and federate derived, protocol-compliant secondary artefacts rather than centralizing raw or sensitive data. It aligns implementations with applicable law including India's DPDP Act 2023 and the EU GDPR, and frames privacy-by-design as simultaneously an ethical obligation and an economic optimization.
