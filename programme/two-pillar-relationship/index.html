The Two-Pillar Relationship — the Civilizational Knowledge Cycle

This document defines the relationship between the two pillars as a continuous civilizational knowledge cycle: preserve, compute, create, reintegrate. Neither pillar is subordinate to or complete without the other; they are coupled through a common canonical computational identity and protocol layer, making the relationship cyclic, symbiotic, and co-dependent rather than hierarchical. Every computational artefact produced by Pillar II re-enters humanity's evolving cultural and scientific corpus and therefore re-enters Pillar I.
