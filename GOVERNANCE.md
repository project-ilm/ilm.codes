# Governance

- **Steward / maintainer:** Abhishek Choudhary (Project ILM founder).
- **Decision basis:** the [Charter](CHARTER.md) and the project's vision, mission, and objectives.
- **Contributions:** via issues and pull requests using the provided templates. Substantive linguistic or
  theoretical contributions are expected to be DOI-backed and, where appropriate, published or patent-filed
  before merge, protecting contributor authorship.
- **Review:** the work speaks — independent validation and invalidation are part of acceptance.
- **Compliance:** maintainers may decline or later remove anything conflicting with the Charter, licensing,
  scope, or safety of participation. Such actions are documented.
- **Licensing:** code GPL-3.0-or-later; docs/posters CC BY 4.0. Contributions must be compatibly licensed.
- **Forking:** anyone may fork and work freely under the licenses. Upstreaming is via PR under this governance.
